## Extracting sensitive information from voice

Your voice messages may reveal more information than you would like them to.

| Information | Accuracy |
| ----------- | -------- |
| Gender      | 97%      |
| Age         | 100%     |
| Ethnicity   | 100%     |
| Income      | 100%     |
| Identity    | 100%     |
